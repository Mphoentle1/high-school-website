ABOUT US

We always strive for success!

Central Secondary is a school which values honour and integrity.
We believe in the importance of knowing what is morally right and responsibility. We aim to teach all of our learners respect,
respect for themselves , others, the school and their environment. With over 1000 pupils,
Central Secondary prides itself in maintaining exceptional academics and providing a holistic education. We believe the key to providing the best
education to our learners is a partnership amongst parents, pupils and staff.

VISION

Our vision is an excellent school where we develop intellectually responsible and
productive citizens of South Africa. We strive to be an institution which rejets any form
of discrimination based on face, colour, gender, religion or culture.

MISSION

Our mission is creating opportunities for learners through the provision of high quality
education via the full utilization of the available resources.Central Secondary is commited
to working in partnership with all the stakeholders of the school.

CSS is commited to ensuring that every learner recieves the best quality education. We will do this by:

Providing the best possible premises and equipment.
Providing the necessary resources to ensure quality education.
Preparing our learners to enter the career of their choice as responsible citizens of society.

WE STRIVE TO CREATE A SAFE LEARNING ENVIRONMENT

We always strive to provide a safe, healthy and secure environment for all our enrollees so that
their gaurdians can meet other responsibilities without worrying about their children's safety.
